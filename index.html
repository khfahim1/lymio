Getting Started

Up & Running in
4 Simple Steps.

No app store. No waiting. Just download, install, and launch your AI companion.

01
⬇️

Download the APK

Tap the golden Download Lymio APK button above or use the direct path ./apk/Lymio.apk. The file downloads instantly via your browser.

02
🔓

Allow Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → Apps → Special App Access → Install Unknown Apps. Toggle permission for your browser (e.g. Chrome). This is a one-time step and safe to do.

03
📲

Install & Launch

Open the downloaded APK file from your notifications or Downloads folder. Tap Install, then Open. Grant the Draw Over Other Apps permission when prompted.

04
🚀

Sign Up & Subscribe

Create your Lymio account inside the app. Navigate to the Subscription tab and choose your plan. Checkout is powered by LemonSqueezy — your account activates within seconds.

Security Note

Lymio does not require root access. All data is encrypted in transit. The overlay permission is used exclusively to render the companion bubble. You can revoke it at any time from your Android settings.
